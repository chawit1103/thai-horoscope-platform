# BETA_LAUNCH_CONTENT_PACK.md - Beta Launch Content Pack

## Goal

Provide approved Thai user-facing copy for beta launch, onboarding, privacy reminders, feedback, and launch announcements.

This pack is communication material only. It does not activate Email, LINE, payment, astro calculation, scheduler, or provider behavior.

## Copy Principles

- Use calm Thai language.
- Frame horoscope content as entertainment and self-reflection.
- Explain that beta behavior is experimental.
- Avoid deterministic promises, fear-based wording, medical claims, legal advice, investment instructions, lottery claims, accident/death predictions, and 100% accuracy claims.
- Do not include raw invite codes, full email addresses, LINE user IDs, birth data, payment identifiers, provider payloads, audit IDs, tokens, API keys, or secrets.
